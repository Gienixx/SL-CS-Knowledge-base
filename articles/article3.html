Not Rewarded

June 16, 2026 By: Genevive Serano

A guide for reviewing survey completion tickets and applying the correct reward resolution.

Article Map

Overview Common User Statements Root Cause Ticket Handling Process Credit Rules Recommended Templates Special Case Agent Checklist Important Reminders Summary

Overview

The Not Rewarded issue occurs when a user completes a survey but does not receive the expected reward because the survey completion did not meet the provider’s or system’s reward eligibility criteria.

This article explains how to identify, review, and resolve tickets from users who report that they were not rewarded after completing a survey.

Common User Statements

Users may describe the issue in different ways, such as:

  • “I was not rewarded for the survey I completed.”
  • “I didn’t get the reward for my survey.”
  • “I finished the survey but the points were not added.”
  • “I completed the offer, but I did not receive my credit.”
  • “Where is my reward for the survey?”

Root Cause

The most common reason for this issue is that the survey completion did not meet the required provider or system criteria for reward eligibility.

A user may have reached the end of a survey experience, but the survey provider may not have marked the completion as valid. This can happen when the user is screened out, fails quality checks, completes the survey outside the required conditions, or when the provider does not confirm the rewardable completion.

Ticket Handling Process

Step 1

Check if the Survey Name Is Mentioned

Review the user’s message and determine whether they provided the name of the survey.

If the survey name is provided, locate the survey in the Survey Table and confirm the reward amount attached to that survey.

If the survey name is not provided, proceed directly to Step 3.

Step 2

Confirm the Reward Amount

When the survey name is available, use the Survey Table to verify the correct reward amount.

Do not rely only on the user’s stated reward amount unless it matches the Survey Table. The Survey Table should be used as the reference for the official reward value.

Step 3

Verify Lifetime Revenue

Next, check the user’s lifetime revenue using the Side Table.

This step is important because the resolution depends on whether the user’s lifetime revenue is below or above the required threshold.

Step 4

Apply the Correct Resolution

Use the user’s lifetime revenue to determine the correct action.

User Lifetime Revenue Resolution
Below $100 Reply using the Not Rewarded template only. Do not issue credit.
Above $100 Reply using the Not Rewarded template and issue a credit.

Credit Rules

When issuing credit, follow these limits:

1

The credit must not exceed the survey’s reward amount.

2

If no survey name is provided, the maximum credit allowed is up to $1.00 only.

3

Credit should only be issued when the user’s lifetime revenue is above $100.

4

Do not issue credit for users below $100 lifetime revenue, even if they claim they completed the survey.

Recommended Response Template

If the User Is Below $100 Lifetime Revenue

Hi [User Name],

Thank you for reaching out. We understand that you completed a survey but did not receive the reward.

After reviewing the activity, the survey did not meet the required provider or system criteria for reward eligibility. Because of this, we are unable to apply a manual credit for this survey.

Please note that survey rewards are only issued when the provider confirms a valid completion. We recommend continuing to complete surveys carefully and making sure all responses are accurate and consistent.

Thank you for your understanding.

If the User Is Above $100 Lifetime Revenue

Hi [User Name],

Thank you for reaching out. We understand that you completed a survey but did not receive the reward.

After reviewing the activity, the survey did not meet the required provider or system criteria for reward eligibility. However, as a courtesy, we have applied a credit to your account.

Please note that survey rewards are normally issued only when the provider confirms a valid completion. Future survey rewards may still depend on provider approval and system eligibility checks.

Thank you for your understanding.

Special Case: No Survey Name Provided

If the user does not provide the survey name, do not delay the ticket unnecessarily. Check the user’s lifetime revenue first.

If the user is below $100, use the Not Rewarded template only.

If the user is above $100, you may issue credit up to $1.00 maximum, then use the Not Rewarded template.

Agent Checklist

Before resolving the ticket, confirm the following:

  • The user is reporting a completed survey with no reward.
  • The survey name was checked, if provided.
  • The Survey Table was used to confirm the reward amount, if applicable.
  • The user’s lifetime revenue was verified using the Side Table.
  • The correct resolution was applied based on lifetime revenue.
  • Any issued credit does not exceed the survey reward amount.
  • If no survey name was provided, the credit does not exceed $1.00.

Important Reminders

Do not promise users that all completed surveys are automatically rewarded. Rewards depend on provider confirmation and system eligibility.

Do not issue credit to users with lifetime revenue below $100.

Do not issue more than the survey’s listed reward amount.

If the survey name is missing, the maximum possible credit is $1.00, and only if the user qualifies based on lifetime revenue.

Summary

For Not Rewarded tickets, the key steps are to check whether the survey name was provided, confirm the reward amount when possible, verify the user’s lifetime revenue, and apply the correct resolution.

Users below $100 lifetime revenue should receive the Not Rewarded template only. Users above $100 lifetime revenue may receive the Not Rewarded template plus a credit, as long as the credit does not exceed the allowed amount.
